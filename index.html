Supply chains: The problem

Supply chains are the networks that move goods from factories to customers — a multi-trillion-dollar system. But these networks are fragile, and hard to model end-to-end.

CatDT: The solution

CatDT is a compositional digital-twin engine that models entire supply-chain networks, delivered as enterprise software that integrates with existing ERP, TMS, and WMS systems.

Why now?

A convergence of nearshoring pressure, post-COVID variability, and new modeling/software capabilities creates an opportunity to define the next generation of supply-chain digital twins.
