CatDT

Composable Digital Twin Architecture for Supply Chain Design

The problem

Supply chains are being redesigned under nearshoring and geopolitical pressure. Existing digital twin and planning tools struggle with structural change: they are siloed, brittle, and difficult to adapt across planning, simulation, and execution layers.

What CatDT is

CatDT is a design-first digital twin engine for supply chains. It provides a compositional architecture that allows supply chain networks to be modeled, simulated, and redesigned in a modular and structurally consistent way.

Who it is for

CatDT is built for supply chain network design teams, strategic planning groups, and organizations undergoing large-scale supply chain transformation.

Status

CatDT is an early-stage product developed by DCat Technologies. The project is founder-led and currently engaging with industry partners for exploratory discussions and pilot opportunities.
